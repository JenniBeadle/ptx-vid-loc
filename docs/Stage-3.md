  - text to translate for slides
    - What is a back translation?  
      1. A way to check the translation.  
      2. In a major language that the consultant understands
      3. In Paratext it is a separate linked project.
    - What are the qualities of a good back translation?
      - Done by someone not part of the translation team.

      - Not use other helps or Bibles
